Privacy Policy for 干点正事儿 Effective Date: 1 June 2026 干点正事儿 is a local task management app based on the Eisenhower Matrix method. Data Collection This app does not require account registration. Task titles, notes, due dates, completion status, reminder settings, and quadrant categories are stored locally on the user's device. The app does not upload task data to our servers. The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. Local Notifications If the user enables reminders, the app may use iOS local notifications. These notifications are generated locally on the user's device. Widgets The app supports Home Screen widgets. Widget data is generated from the user's local task data and remains on the device.
